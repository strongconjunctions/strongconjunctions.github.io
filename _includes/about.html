I am a UI/UX designer. Most of the things I make are user oriented. This means that my design process is geared towards a specific client, and a specific purpose. Design for me is first and foremost about functionality at every level: at the level of performance, functionality, code readability, ease of access, and visual simplicity. I therefore view myself as someone heavily invested in such design features as organization, planning, and taxonomies. Design is also effective information distribution, hence any highly organized and efficient system is always a good designed and beautiful system. I also strongly believe that any high order of organization is inherently beautiful, as it lacks any unnecessary parts.
